## Abstract
Short text clustering has become increasingly important with social media popularity. This work proposes an enhanced model-based approach, combining topic model-based and deep representation learning paradigms.

## Core Method

### 1. Two Paradigms in Short Text Clustering
- **Topic model-based approaches**: GSDMM, LDA variants
- **Deep representation learning**: Neural embeddings, transformers

### 2. Enhanced Model-based Approach
- Combines strengths of both paradigms
- Addresses sparsity issues in short texts
- Improved coherence and discriminability

### 3. Key Challenges Addressed
- Low word co-occurrence in short texts
- Semantic understanding with limited context
- Scalability to large social media datasets

## Relationship to Our Research
- Relevant for text preprocessing and topic modeling
