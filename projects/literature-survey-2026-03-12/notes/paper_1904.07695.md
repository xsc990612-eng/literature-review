## Abstract
Survey of short text topic modeling techniques. Traditional methods (PLSA, LDA) fail due to lack of word co-occurrence information in short texts.

## Core Method

### 1. Problem Statement
- Traditional topic models (PLSA, LDA) rely on word co-occurrences
- Short texts lack sufficient co-occurrence information
- Need specialized techniques for short text topic modeling

### 2. Method Categories
- **Generative models**: GSDMM, BTM, WNTM
- **Embedding-based**: TopicVec, LFTM
- **Neural approaches**: NTM, BATM

### 3. Key Techniques
- GSDMM (Gibbs Sampling Dirichlet Multinomial Mixture)
- Biterm Topic Model (BTM)
- Word Network Topic Model
